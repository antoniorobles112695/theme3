Me, Myself, And I

This... is me.

Food is an easy way to make you not think of other things. It limits your stress and makes you even happier at certain moments. The good thing about food is that it makes me unaware of the possible or real problems that I have to face everyday.

I'am a type of guy who likes to overthink (for some reason). Sometimes, I struggle to get my mind off things that I seriously don't want to think about. Unfortunately, it keeps coming back the second I forget about it. It's actually not food alone that helps me forget about certain things, there are actually a lot more that I'd rather not talk about.

But, it does seem that food is probably the "safest" way to forget about your problems. Well, until you eat a lot and get fat. But it does help you over come many struggles and challenges in your life.

So, this is me. A person who cannot bare problems that I cannot face. A person who's just really trying to branch out to find out who I really am and what really is my purpose here in this world. Luckily, food is there to help me out... at least whenever I need it, it will always be right there by my side.
